Palettes aim to align common color usages across interfaces. All of the colors used in our palettes come from spectrum colors. Palette colors should be used before spectrum colors, as they were rigorously tested in various scenarios to ensure:

- Accessibility & consistency
- A seamless switch between light and dark modes
- Easily themeable by an alternative brand

### Foreground

This part of the palette is used when applying color to text & icons or other content. The palette utilizes ensures contrast in light and dark mode
